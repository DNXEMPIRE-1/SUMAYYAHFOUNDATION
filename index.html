Non-profit Charity Organization

We are a community-based organization started in 2021 with an aim of providing legal aid services, provision of Sexual Reproductive Health Rights (SRHR) services, economic empowerment towards vulnerable boys, Adolescent Girls and Young Women (AGYW) in Kenya.
To facilitate a community-based collaborative response to all survivors of Gender Based Violence (GBV) and Sexual Gender-Based Violence (SGBV) by providing medical, advocacy and legal aid services in an ethical and compassionate manner.
To decolonize the societal norms and systems geared towards Gender Based Violence (GBV) and Sexual Gender-Based Violence (SGBV) against vulnerable boys, Adolescent Girls and Young Women (AGYW) in Kenya

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

We provide nutritious meals to ensure everyone has access to the food they need for a healthy life. Our programs focus on fighting hunger and promoting balanced diets, especially for children and vulnerable communities.

Pure Water

Access to clean drinking water is a basic human right. We work to install water purification systems, dig wells, and ensure sustainable water sources in areas that lack clean water, preventing waterborne diseases and improving overall health.

Health Care

Quality healthcare should be accessible to all. We offer medical services, health check-ups, vaccinations, and health education to underserved populations. Our initiatives aim to reduce illness and mortality rates, particularly among children and the elderly.

Primary Education

Education is the foundation for a better future. We support primary education by building schools, providing learning materials, and training teachers. Our goal is to give every child the opportunity to learn and grow in a safe and supportive environment.

Residence Facilities

Safe and stable housing is crucial for well-being. We provide shelters and work on housing projects to ensure that families have a secure place to live. Our efforts focus on rebuilding homes in disaster-stricken areas and improving living conditions in impoverished communities.

Social Care

We offer a range of social care services to support individuals and families in need. This includes counseling, job training, and assistance programs that help people overcome challenges and improve their quality of life.
